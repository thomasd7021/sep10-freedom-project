Software

Most of the softwear electrical engineers use are things to simulate circuits and help print things.

Electrical Technology Android App

It is an app that Can be used offline, Has calculators specifically for Electrical engineers, Has free tutorials to learn Electrical engineering and it's free.

ElectroDroid

ElectroDroid is another app hat Has color codes (for wires), Calculators, Has schematics and is Free but has a pro version

ETAP

ETAP is a application that allows Electrical engineers to simulate cities and analyze them

EveryCircuit

EveryCircuit is another apps that Helps with understanding circuits and how to make them, Lets you build circuits, Has animations to help comprehension and its also Free

ECAD

ECAD is a 3d builder that Electrical engineers use to build circuit boards and circuits to be seen.
